Chicken Wings

Home

Description

Chicken Wings are tasty foods that can be crispy, delicious , flavorful, and most important of all, easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Oil: Locks the moisture and makes the seasoning stick
  • Seasoning: For the extra taste on the Wings
  • Wings: Need 10 Wings for this recipe

Steps

  1. Combine oil and seasoning together and blend together
  2. Add the wings to the mix and make sure it is well coated
  3. Arrange on a baking sheet and bake until crispy
